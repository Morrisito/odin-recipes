Ground Chicken Taco Bowls

Ground Chicken Taco Bowls paired with shredded cabbage, rice, and a side of salsa roja. This is a flavorful and filling dish for under 500 calories.

Ingredients

For the meat

  • 2 lbs (908 g) ground chicken (95/5)
  • 3/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp lumin
  • 1/2 tsp oil

For Cabbage

  • 12 oz shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt & pepper to taste

For Rice

  • 563g Cooked Rice
  • 1/2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 60g toma tomatic diced

For the Salsa Roja

  • 240g roma tomatoes
  • 100g white onion
  • 60g jalapenos
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp oil
  • 20g cilantro
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • salt to taste
