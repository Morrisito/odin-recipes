LASAGNA

4 cheese lasagna with extra meat

This lasagna is so good it will melt in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • any cheese you want, recommened are cheddar, mozzarella, swiss & blue cheese
  • any meat, preferably beef or chicken
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • and ofc lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. boil pasta
  2. grate your cheese while the pasta boils
  3. cook your beef, but do not fully cook it
  4. add everything together and and baek at 350
